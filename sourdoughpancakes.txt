Sourdough Pancakes
Source: 


Ingredients:
Overnight Sponge:
* 100g sourdough starter (unfed discard)
* 125g all-purpose flour
* 170g butterbilk
* 12g sugar

Batter:
* All of the overnight sponge
* 1 large egg
* 2 tbsp melted butter
* 24g sugar
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 3/4 tsp kosher salt
* 1/2 tsp baking soda

Method:
For the overnight sponge mix all ingredients together and let rest for 12 hours or until risen to twice its size on the kitchen counter.

1. Heat pan or griddle to medium / high heat ~350F
2. Melt butter and let it cool
3. Beat egg, sugar, salt and vanilla extract together
4. Whisk in cooled melted butter
5. Carefully fold into overnight sponge
6. Stir in baking soda and wait 10 - 20 seconds
7. Ladel batter onto griddle, flipping after a few minutes to cook both sides

Serve with maple syrup.
