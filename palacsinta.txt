Palacsinta
Serves: 4
Time: ?
Work time: ?

Ingredients:
* 150g flour
* 150ml milk
* 3 tsp water
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 egg + 1 egg yolk

Method:
1. Sift flour and salt together
2. Make a well in the middle and add the one egg and egg yolk
3. Mix milk and water together
4. Start stirring together ingredients
5. Put the mix in fridge for at least an hour
6. Ladel a bit in to hot pan and cook on both sides

Toppings:
I tend to go for Nesquick chocolate powder or jam (appricot or strawberry)
